What's next, after you log in
  1. Settings → Adapter Management — assign roles to your USB adapters (wlan-mgmt-ap, wlan-mon-2g, etc.), click Apply. Sticky names take effect immediately, no reboot needed.
  2. Settings → Networking → Host AP on interface — move the management AP from wlan0 to wlan-mgmt-ap so wlan0 is freed for upstream Wi-Fi.
  3. Settings → Networking → Scan — find your home Wi-Fi, click Connect. Now you have upstream and a fallback path back to the platform.
  4. Settings → Networking → Management AP — finally, change the AP SSID/password away from the bootstrap defaults.
